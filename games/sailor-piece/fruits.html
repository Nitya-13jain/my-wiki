🍇 Obtainment

Players can obtain fruits from the Coin Fruit Dealer or Gem Fruit Dealer by spending the respective type of currency. Fruits can be dropped manually at any time by selecting them directly inside your hotbar/inventory slot layout and pressing the Backspace key binding.
